02 · Trip phases
How the Trip is Structured
Phase 1 · Jun 27–29
Jackson Hole & Grand Teton
3 nights · Teton Mountain Lodge & Spa
Land in Jackson Hole, pick up both rental cars, tour the town, catch the Jackson Rodeo, and spend two full days exploring Grand Teton National Park.
Phase 2 · Jun 30–Jul 2
Yellowstone National Park
3 nights · Canyon Lodge & Cabins
Drive 2 hrs 15 min north to Yellowstone. Three full days to explore geysers, hot springs, wildlife, and the Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone.
Phase 3 · Jul 3–4
Missoula — Rehearsal & Wedding
2 nights · Residence Inn Missoula Downtown
Drive 4 hrs 20 min west to Missoula. Friday rehearsal, then the wedding on Independence Day, July 4th.
Phase 4 · Jul 5
Departure from Bozeman
Drive 3 hrs · drop cars · fly home
Morning drive to Bozeman, return both rental cars, and split: one group flies home to CLT, another heads to San Diego for the ADF Conference (Jul 5–10).
